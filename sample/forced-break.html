Forced Page Breaks

Demonstrating the pdfpagebreak class

This short document fits comfortably on a single page. The pages that follow exist only because of explicit page breaks added in the markup — not because the content overflowed.

To force a break, add class="pdfpagebreak" to any element: that element will begin on a new page. You can also drop in an empty <div class="pdfpagebreak"></div> as a standalone marker.

Section Two (starts on its own page)

This heading carries the pdfpagebreak class, so it begins on page 2 even though there was plenty of room left on page 1. Everything that follows flows normally until the next forced break.
